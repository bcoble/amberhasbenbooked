Amber Stratman

bride

Amber grew up in the tiny town of Shiner, TX with her two big sisters and her parents. In highschool she could usually be found playing volleyball, acting in plays or leading a marching band while dressed as a pirate. As an adult, she loves going to broadway musicals, cats, reading, creative hobbies such as painting, sculpting, woodworking, etc. Her passion for all things creative led her to her dream job as a Designer for Keller Williams Realty where she met many lifelong friends. Her favorite color is blue. Particularly the blue of her fiance's beautiful blue eyes. ☺️ Also... don't challenge her at Harry Potter trivia. You will lose.

Ben Coble

groom

Ben moved to 5 different states with his parents and younger brother before finding their way to their home in Melbourne, Florida. Growing up you could find Ben playing video games, endlessly reading or creating mischief with his buddies on the beach. 5 years later he graduated first in his class from Melbourne High School. He always had a knack for technology, math and science (not to mention video games) which naturally led him down the path of double majoring in math and computer science at Rose Hulman University to become a software engineer. Today he has a great love for books and hopes to one day design and build a video game from scratch.
